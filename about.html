About

Hello, my name is Howie Strang

My goal is to become a 3D Artist; working for a video game studio - big or small. I really want to be apart of the video game industry whether I make something minor or something major, I'd be happy to have been apart of a video game project.

I have been working with 3DS Max Studio for 3 years now. I mostly focus on creating objects such as tables, computers, and buildings. I'm a great illustrator, I've been drawing since I was little, mostly self-taught and I've continued to draw and still improving my craft. I love working with Adobe PhotoShop because over the years of being in Confederation Colleges, I've learned how to bring my illustrations to a digital space and learning most of the tools that's needed for illustrating. Adobe After Effects, I've worked with for 2 years though not consistent, I always use After Effects for video editing instead of Premiere because of how much I am able to edit and do animations on one software.
